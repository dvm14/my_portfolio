Hi! I'm an AI/ML Engineer pursuing my MEng in AI and Product Innovation at Duke University, where I study modeling algorithms, data processing, and AI in the physical world.

Currently, I'm exploring object detection in images and diving into explainability in AI to make models more transparent and trustworthy. I'm also integrating AI with Raspberry Pi to bring intelligence to edge devices. As Vice President of Duke's AI Competition Club, I host the university's annual AI hackathon and provide a space for students to bring their ideas to life.

Before Duke, I worked as a Software Engineer at Cisco, building network security solutions for next-generation firewalls.

I'm passionate about making physical products that help people and give back to under-resourced communities, making people's lives better instead of easier.

When I'm not training models or writing code, you can find me watching fun movies, trying new beers and restaurants, traveling to new places, or playing pool with friends :)
